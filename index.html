The all new 5090 lighting is here!

The POWER Lightnning 5090 is a flagship GPU featuring 21,760 CUDA cores, 32 GB GDDR7 memory, and advanced AI and ray tracing capabilities. Core Architecture and GPU The Lightning 5090 is built on NVIDIAs Blackwell architecture using the GB202-300-A1 GPU. It features 170 Streaming Multiprocessors (SMs) out of 192, totaling 21,760 CUDA cores. The GPU includes 680 Tensor cores for AI acceleration and 170 RT cores for hardware ray tracing, supporting DirectX 12 Ultimate and advanced rendering features. The die size is 750 mm² with 92.2 billion transistors
